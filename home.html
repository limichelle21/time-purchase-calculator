The True Cost of Shopping

Have you ever considered the true cost of what you're buying?

We've always heard the term 'Time is Money'.

So what if you considered the cost of shopping in terms of hours instead of dollars?



How to calculate your hourly wage: Divide your annual salary by total number of hours worked. There are 2080 hours in a standard 40-hour workweek per year. For more realistic estimates, enter your after-tax hourly wage.
